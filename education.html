High School (2015-2017)

NCSSM was a one-of-a-kind high school experience. It's a public boarding school down the street from Duke that accepts rising juniors (~350 per class) from across NC for their last 2 years of high school of intensive STEM curriculum and research. I took a lot of college level chemistry, biology, physics, math, and engineering courses. I also did 4 trimesters of materials chemistry and nanomaterials research as part of the Research in Chemistry program (where only 12 students were selected).

Select Coursework: Research in Chemistry I-IV; Organic Chemistry; Materials Chemistry; Medicinal Chemistry; Biochemistry; Graph Theory & Proofs; Multivariable Calculus; Mathematical Modeling; AP Biology I-III; AP Music Theory; Biomedical Engineering; Advanced Physics
